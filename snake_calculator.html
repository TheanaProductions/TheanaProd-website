Snake Calculator is a simple way for young children to learn simple 1, 2, and 3 additions, all while having fun.


Simply choose a difficulty, and let your children try to reach the goal by adding either 1, 2, or 3 to their scores. Going higher, or colliding with the snake body or walls will stop the game.


Available in English, French and Portuguese (Brazil).

If you are looking for a normal Snake game, then you can check our Classic Snake game.
