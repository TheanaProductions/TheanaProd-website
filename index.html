What is Theana Productions?

Theana Productions is a French base game dev company created in September 2021.

It is a place for us to create interesting and enjoyable games for everyone. We aim on improving game accessibility in order to create a new era of game... and hopefully a better future for everyone thanks to our games.

As of today, we mostly create 2D games for Android, Windows, and Linux. All of those can be found here.


We wish you a good visit on our website, and we hope you'll enjoy playing our games as much as we enjoyed making them!
