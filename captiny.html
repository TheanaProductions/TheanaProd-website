Explore, build upgrade and become rich in this RPG meet Simulation game.


  • Choose between multiple biomes to start your journey
  • Explore different biomes to forage what you need
  • Meet a bunch of adorable NPCs
  • Create your own base village
  • Go down in the dungeons and test your strength against the local monsters
  • Win rewards and dress your little character as you wish
  • Multiple difficulty levels for those who prefer to rest and those who want to fight
  • And so much more...
