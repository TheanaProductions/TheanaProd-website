Explore the different biomes and learn more about Captiny's world in this sweet RPG/Exploration game.


  • Choose between multiple biomes to start your journey
  • Explore different biomes to forage what you need
  • Meet a bunch of adorable NPCs
  • Decorate your house
  • Test your strength against the local monsters
  • Win rewards and dress your little character as you wish
