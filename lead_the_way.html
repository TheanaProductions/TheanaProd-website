If you ask Bob the blob what they think about being with your loved one, they'll probably tell you that it is the best feeling in the world.


That's what they feel when they are with their partner.


Unfortunately, because of a strange turn of events, they ended up being separated in a dark maze. It is now up to Bob to brave the dangers of the maze in order to be reunited with their loved one.


  • Simple gameplay, perfect to relax or pass the time
  • A cute character that will with no doubt make you fall in love
  • A hard mode for those who like the challenge
  • 40 levels to test your abilities
