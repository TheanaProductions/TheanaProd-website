When Mary woke up this morning, it wasn't to the sound of laughs and idle chatter of her fairy comrades, but the explosive and deafening one of a failed experimentation.

Once again, she prayed for luck to be on her side, but once again, it seemed that her prayers had stayed unanswered as she soon realized she was the last fairy in her village.


Help Mary in her quest to discover what went wrong this night and hopefully get her friends back in this sweetly designed platform game.

  • A well-written story that will make you want to know more
  • Cute and relaxing designs that will make you feel better instantly
