1. What is Hide and Seek?

Hide and Seek is a simple 2D luck game made for mobile and web browsers. It is available respectively on PlayStore and Itch.io. It was created and is still currently a product from Theana Productions, a French company.

2. What information do we collect?

Hide and Seek doesn’t need to collect any information in order to run correctly.
